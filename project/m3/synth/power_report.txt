## Timing Violation Note

The full PnR flow completed all 44 steps including routing, DRC check
(0 violations), and LVS (0 errors), but reported setup timing violations
at the 4.0 ns (250 MHz) clock target. The worst negative slack is
-3.52 ns, and the tool suggests a clock period of 14.0 ns (71.4 MHz)
for the current implementation. This indicates the critical path through
the av_unit 32-bit multiplier is too long for 250 MHz without additional
pipelining. See critical_path.md for the fix plan.

---

## Power Summary

The design consumes approximately **5.18 mW** total at typical corner,
with 3.40 mW internal (65.6%) and 1.78 mW switching (34.4%). Leakage
is negligible at 15 nW. This is a reasonable power level for a
synthesized attention accelerator pipeline on Sky130A at 71.4 MHz.
