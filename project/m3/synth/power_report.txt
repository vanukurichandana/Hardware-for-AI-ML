# Power Estimation Report — M3
## Tool: OpenLane 1.1.1 | Design: top | PDK: Sky130A

Power estimation via OpenLane 1 synthesis exploration does not produce
a standalone power report. Full power analysis requires placement and
routing (PnR) which is beyond the synthesis-only flow used in M3.

## What Was Attempted

OpenLane 1.1.1 was run with the -synth_explore flag on the top module
(full attention pipeline + AXI4-Stream interface) targeting Sky130A PDK
at 4.0 ns clock period. The synthesis flow completed successfully with
0 problems, producing area and timing reports. However, OpenLane 1
synthesis exploration does not invoke OpenSTA power analysis — that
step requires a placed and routed netlist.

## Estimated Power (Static Analysis)

Based on Sky130A standard cell characterization data:
- Dynamic power scales approximately as: P = α × C × V² × f
- Activity factor α ≈ 0.1 (typical for pipelined datapath)
- Supply voltage V = 1.8V (Sky130A nominal)
- Clock frequency f = 250 MHz
- Total cell count: 2,500 cells (AREA_0 strategy)
- Estimated capacitance: ~50 fF per cell average

Estimated dynamic power ≈ 0.1 × 2500 × 50fF × (1.8)² × 250MHz
                         ≈ 0.1 × 2500 × 50e-15 × 3.24 × 250e6
                         ≈ **10.1 mW (estimated)**

Static leakage power for Sky130A at nominal corner:
  ~1 nW per cell × 2500 cells ≈ **2.5 μW (leakage)**

## Plan for M4

For M4, power estimation will be attempted using:
1. OpenLane full PnR flow to generate a placed and routed netlist
2. OpenSTA power analysis on the post-PnR netlist
3. If full PnR fails, use Yosys power estimation from gate-level netlist

The estimated 10.1 mW dynamic power will be validated against the
post-PnR OpenSTA result in M4.
