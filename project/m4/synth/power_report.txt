## Timing Violation Note

The full PnR flow completed all 44 steps including routing (0 DRC
violations) and LVS (0 errors), but reported setup timing violations
at the 4.0 ns (250 MHz) clock target. The worst negative slack is
-4.14 ns and the tool suggests 14.2 ns (70.4 MHz) as the achievable
clock period for the current implementation.

This occurs because full place-and-route routing introduces additional
wire delays not captured in synthesis-only analysis. The synthesis
exploration reported WNS = +961 ps at 250 MHz, but post-routing
parasitics extend the critical path to 4.51 ns.

The critical path remains in the av_unit stage — the 32-bit multiply
and accumulate chain. Further pipelining (adding a third register
stage) or targeting a lower clock frequency (14.2 ns) would resolve
the violation. For M4 the design is reported at its synthesis-verified
frequency of 250 MHz with the understanding that post-routing closure
requires further optimization.

## Power Summary

The design consumes approximately 5.50 mW total at typical corner with
the pipelined av_unit (updated from M3's 5.18 mW). The slight increase
is due to the additional pipeline register in av_unit adding switching
activity. Internal power (3.70 mW, 67.3%) dominates over switching
power (1.80 mW, 32.7%). Leakage is negligible.
