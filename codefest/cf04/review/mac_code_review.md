## Issue 1: Invalid Casting Syntax (mac_llm_B.v)

**Offending line:**
out <= out + 32'($signed(a) * $signed(b));

**Explanation:**
The syntax `32'(...)` is not valid in SystemVerilog. Proper casting must follow the format `type'(expression)`. This results in non-synthesizable and non-standard code.

**Corrected version:**
out <= out + (a * b);

---

## Issue 2: Redundant Signed Casting

**Offending line:**
$signed(a) * $signed(b)

**Explanation:**
Inputs `a` and `b` are already declared as signed. Applying `$signed()` is unnecessary and reduces readability.

**Corrected version:**
out <= out + (a * b);

---

## Issue 3: Missing Explicit Sign Extension

**Explanation:**
The product of two 8-bit signed values is 16-bit, while the accumulator is 32-bit. Without explicit sign extension, the accumulation behavior may be ambiguous depending on synthesis tools.

**Corrected version:**
logic signed [15:0] product;
assign product = a * b;
out <= out + {{16{product[15]}}, product};
